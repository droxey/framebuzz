MANIFESTO

FrameBuzz is a free, online video conversation platform that enables moment-by-moment discussions within a video. As the facilitator of interesting conversations between content creators, experts, and consumers, FrameBuzz brings more to a conversation than just words – it brings meaningful connections and unique perspectives that help shape the way a video is perceived, shared, and discovered.

FrameBuzz brings comments into the moment.

We facilitate meaningful, relevant conversations around video.

  • Who We Are

    We’re social. We’re engaged. We’re right there with you. We’re FrameBuzz. Born out of co-founders Aaron Lindsey and Jason Kohn’s never-ending excitement for great content and passionate debate, we’re helping you curate that online conversation, frame by frame.

  • What We Believe

    At FrameBuzz we believe that video viewers are more than just people – they’re influencers. They have valuable opinions that deserve to be shared, and through their individual comments, praise, questions, and curated conversations, those viewers have the ability to transform a video from content to something so much more.

  • Why We’re Different

    For us, it’s more than just leaving a comment that’s never to be seen again. It’s about crafting meaningful dialogue in the most intuitive way that has a chance to change a video’s perspective and shape its direction. We’re helping to create a community of passionate words, dynamic videos and conversations that matter.
