Using FrameBuzz

  • Interact

    FrameBuzz acts like a chat room in an online video, creating an opportunity for 1:1 communication between the video creator and viewers. It’s as if you were watching together, moderating, asking and answering questions, and gaining insight about what’s most interesting in the video, but without having to watch at the same time.

  • Discover

    View and comment in top FrameBuzzed videos from around the web. Follow interesting people who will lead you to new, relevant video content. Never again go wading through the fire hose of social media to find relevant comments because now they’re right there in the video player, attached to that specific moment in the video. And our heat map at the bottom of each video helps you jump ahead to the juicy parts.

  • Influence

    FrameBuzz gives people a way to not just comment but question, debate, protest, praise and ultimately shape video content for the better. For websites with video, FrameBuzz provides a view into what’s happening on the site with valuable feedback to help strengthen content and engage with viewers.

  • Enjoy

    FrameBuzz your favorite videos on your site or ours, and invite friends to join in the conversation with you. It’s the electronic version of a watch party with your smartest friends, with the added bonus that the director might join in the debate.
